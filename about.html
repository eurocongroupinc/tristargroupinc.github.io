History & Leadership

Our Origin

Forged in Precision.

Founded in 1995, Tristar Group began as a humble tool room in the suburbs of Mumbai. Under the visionary leadership of our founders, we identified a gap in the Indian market for global-standard mould manufacturing.

Today, we stand as a vertically integrated manufacturing powerhouse. We don't just make parts; we engineer solutions that power the automotive, medical, and consumer electronics sectors across 15 countries.
